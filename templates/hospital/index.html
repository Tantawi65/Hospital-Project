Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Hospital Management System

A Hospital Management System is software that helps hospitals and clinics manage their daily operations. It keeps track of patient records, doctor appointments, billing, inventory, and staff schedules.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online by clicking the "Book an Appointment" button on our homepage and make an account.

Does the system generate and manage patient bills?

Yes, the system automatically generates patient bills based on consultations, treatments, tests, and medications.

Can doctors update patient records directly in the system?

Yes, doctors can update patient records directly in the system.
